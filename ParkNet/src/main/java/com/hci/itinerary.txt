#### Day 1: April 25, 2024 – Arrival at Great Smoky Mountains National Park

*** 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM
- Arrive at Great Smoky Mountains National Park and check-in to the hotel or campsite, depending on your accommodation preference.

*** 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM
- Take a leisurely walk around the hotel or campsite area to get familiar with the surroundings.
- Enjoy the fresh mountain air and beautiful scenery.

*** 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM
- Have dinner at a local restaurant nearby.
- After dinner, relax and unwind at your accommodation, or take a stroll around the nearby area.

#### Day 2: April 26, 2024 – Great Smoky Mountains Exploration

*** 8:00 AM - 10:00 AM
- Start the day with a hearty breakfast at your accommodation, hotel or campsite.
- Gather any information or maps you may need for your exploration.

*** 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM
- Visit the Sugarlands Visitor Center to learn about the history and wildlife of Great Smoky Mountains National Park.
- Take a guided nature walk or attend a Ranger-led program to enhance your knowledge.

*** 12:00 PM - 2:00 PM
- Enjoy a picnic lunch at one of the scenic picnic areas within the park.
- Take in the stunning views and maybe have a peaceful nature walk.

*** 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM
- Drive to Cades Cove, a popular area in the park known for its historic buildings and abundant wildlife.
- Explore the various trails and take a bicycle ride around the loop road.

*** 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM
- Head back to your accommodation and freshen up.
- Relax and enjoy the amenities offered by your accommodation, such as a swimming pool or spa.

*** 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM
- Have dinner at a local restaurant in the nearby town.
- Enjoy a peaceful evening watching the sunset from a scenic viewpoint within the park.

#### Day 3: April 27, 2024 – Grand Smoky Mountains Adventure

*** 8:00 AM - 10:00 AM
- Start the day with a delicious breakfast at your accommodation.
- Gather any necessary gear or equipment for outdoor activities.

*** 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM
- Take a scenic drive along Newfound Gap Road to reach the highest point in the park.
- Enjoy the panoramic views and stop at various overlooks for photo opportunities.

*** 12:00 PM - 2:00 PM
- Have a picnic lunch at one of the designated picnic areas with breathtaking mountain views.
- Take some time to relax and soak in the natural beauty of the area.

*** 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM
- Choose from a variety of activities such as hiking, fishing, or horseback riding, depending on your preference and accessibility.
- Explore some of the iconic trails within the park, such as the Appalachian Trail or Laurel Falls Trail.

*** 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM
- Return to your accommodation and freshen up.
- Spend some time relaxing and reflecting on the incredible experiences of the day.

*** 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM
- Have dinner at a local restaurant in the nearby town.
- Enjoy a peaceful evening by the campfire, sharing stories and stargazing.

#### Day 4: April 28, 2024 – Departure from Great Smoky Mountains National Park

*** 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM
- Check out from your accommodation and prepare for departure.
- Pack up your belongings and ensure you have everything you need for the journey back.

*** 11:00 AM - 1:00 PM
- Take a scenic drive through the park, savoring the final views of the majestic mountains.
- Stop at any spots of interest along the way for one last photo opportunity.

*** 1:00 PM - 3:00 PM
- Have a leisurely lunch at a local restaurant on the outskirts of the park.
- Relax and reminisce about the memorable experiences you had during your trip.

*** 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM
- Begin your journey back to Tallahassee, FL, or your next destination.
- Drive safely and enjoy the scenic surroundings during the return trip.

*** 5:00 PM - 7:00 PM
- Arrive back in Tallahassee, FL, or your preferred destination, marking the end of your Great Smoky Mountains adventure.
